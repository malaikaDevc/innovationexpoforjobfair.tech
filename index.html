About Us

The Innovation Expo Job Fair is an annual event organized by ACM Society in Khawaja Fareed University of Engineering and Information Technology. With a mission to connect aspiring professionals with leading industry players, startups, and innovators, we aim to create a dynamic platform for networking, learning, and career advancement.

  • At the Innovation Expo, expect dynamic opportunities in jobs, tech, discussions, and workshops.

  • Our vision is to inspire, connect, and innovate, empowering the next generation of leaders.

  • Join us at the Innovation Expo Job Fair for exploration, empowerment, and welcoming all.

Why Attend This

Networking Opportunities

At the Innovation Expo, engage in meaningful networking with industry professionals, clients, collaborators, and like-minded individuals. Forge genuine connections and partnerships, advancing your career and business prospects.

Empowering Success

Empowering success is central to the Innovation Expo. Through networking, learning, and career advancement, we provide tools to turn aspirations into reality. Join us on this journey to achieve new heights together.

Job And Career Opportunities

At the Innovation Expo, we prioritize job and career opportunities. For job seekers, our expos offer access to job openings, on-the-spot interviews, and career workshops to enhance skills and marketability. Join us to explore new career paths and seize exciting opportunities!

Tech Innovations And Ideas

Explore cutting-edge technology at the Innovation Expo. Engage with passionate entrepreneurs showcasing groundbreaking innovations. Join us and be part of the next wave of tech advancement!
